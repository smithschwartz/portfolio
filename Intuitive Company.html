Intuitive Who?

Why Does Intuitive Company Work for YOU?

We’re successful because we’re passionate about providing:

• Elegant, useful, and enjoyable user experiences
• Technology that people need and want, where they need and want it
• Value to our clients’ technological investments
• The maximum amount of impact on our clients’ end user audience
• Genuine, productive, and lasting partnerships

Who is IC?

We design and build web applications, mobile apps, responsive web sites, and desktop software that improve communication, reduce training, and increase productivity and user adoption. IC is a user centered design firm that takes pride in making sure a product is USABLE.

Pretty is pretty useless without function.

We trust our instincts (so should you, just saying). Our varied skills and expertise allow us to be attentive and nimble to project, client, and user needs. At the core, we are a rag tag collection of:

  • Researchers
  • Information Architects
  • Technologists
  • Designers
  • Usability Strategists
  • Project Managers

Based on our clients’ needs, we assemble a team of experts whose depth of skills and ingenuity is unparalleled. We’ve worked for companies both large, small, and in just about every industry. We’ve proven to be the best at streamlining processes and making users happy.

Decades of experience in user-centered design, process improvement, and technology implementation provide our team of diverse specialists inside IC the ability to approach projects with insight and perspective.

Contact us to learn more about how we can partner together and build the best possible experience for your users.

Stuff we’re good at

Stuff we’re good at

What do we do?

We balance user needs with the needs of your business. Our experience allows us to find that right balance between innovation and usability. By leveraging available and new technologies, we create something useful, usable, and engaging.

  • Applications

    Whether applications are running your business or bringing your business money, they’re everywhere. We’ve successfully designed web, desktop, enterprise, and mobile applications. Our designs can optimize productivity, reduce employee training, increase communication, and improve adoption. We can also help create consistent experiences to ensure familiar and productive activities for your users.

  • Mobile, Tablet, & Responsive

    Did you know that there’s expected to be over 1 billion smart phone users in the next year? That’s too big to ignore. We can design responsive mobile web applications to accommodate any size screen. We can also go native and make the most of smart phone technology. Bottom line, we design and build mobile experiences that work for your business and your users.

  • Websites

    A website isn’t just a website anymore, it is a call to action. Whether it’s to shop, log on to a secure experience, or act as a gateway to learn more, the point of your site is not just to have a great brand. We help you define your site goals and design accordingly. And no matter what, we believe website design should be responsive to ensure your users see what you want them to see on any device.

  • Research

    We don’t just guess what your application needs, we specialize in learning that from your users. To do this, we incorporate our Researchers and Usability Strategists into the design process from start to finish. We do the research and validate it every step of the way, up to and including interviews, usability testing and findings presentations. It’s a comprehensive and necessary service, making sure that you get the very best experience out of your application. If it’s broke, we’ll figure out why before we fix it.

  • Technology & Development

    We build stuff, too! We can manage and plan the next release, help your team build a release, schedule a design sprint with multiple iterations, or if you want, we can develop it for you. Our Technology Developers, Designers, and Information Architects ensure that you receive the most functional experience. Current and future technologies are taken into account and incorporated into everything we deliver. Our goal is to provide the most effective and adaptable end result.

  • Strategy

    With the aid of comprehensive usability research, we can formulate an action plan and guide you down a user-friendly path of success. Whether it’s visioning a direction for pre-existing applications or creating a road map for the next concept, our Usability Strategists and Designers work hand-in-hand to deliver a user-centered experience.

How do we do all this?

We understand that not one process fits all, so we can mix and match deliverables and techniques that meet your needs.

  • Business Strategy

    At the start of any engagement, we partner with your organization to gain an understanding of you. We want to understand how you do business, the goals of your organization, and the systems and processes in place that support those objectives.

  • Usability Strategy

    We apply both user research methods and usability best practices, allowing our team to understand the behaviors and attitudes of your users. We obtain this understanding through interviews, contextual inquiries/observations, and usability testing resulting in detailed findings that define the expectations and desires of the end user. This allows our Usability Strategists to operate as user advocates in the design process. It’s a vital and invaluable process because the user cannot be forgotten in the design.

  • Information Design

    Every sturdy structure begins with a blueprint. The focus of information design is to define the data required on a screen and its relationship to data found elsewhere. The manifestation of information design includes information architecture and/or hierarchy diagrams, wireframes, and info-graphics.

  • Interaction Design

    Interaction design is vital to ensure a consistent user experience across any application or website. The focus is to ensure that all actions across a site (from submit buttons to navigation behavior) are easy to use. Interaction design begins during information design and continues through to the application of aesthetics.

  • Visual Design

    Visual design is often described as the overall “look and feel.” In delivering visual design, we consider your company’s brand, the use of meaningful colors, graphical elements, and page layouts to enhance the overall usability of the end product.

  • Pattern Design

    Pattern design is critical in establishing standards and re-using controls within a large application. Patterns help maintain a consistent user experience and reduce development costs. Often, a core set of screens and screen components can be designed and delivered for use across an application without having to design each and every screen.

  • Technology Development

    Building the user experience to match the integrity of the designs and to work flawlessly for the users is key to our Technology Development efforts. We’re pushing the latest proven standards like HTML5, CSS3, and responsive solutions to work on any user’s myriad of devices. Our goal is to find the best way to future-proof your investment with innovation.
