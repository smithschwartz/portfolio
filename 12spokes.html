About Us

We are Ruby on Rails specialists, and we love it.

SmithSchwartz Front-End Developer

Passionate about the tiniest of details, Smith is always looking for the most creatively concise way to make things better. Whether it's quibbling over CSS or pushing pixels in Photoshop, she is usually found tinkering about the front-end designing and building useful, delightful interfaces.

A nomad at heart, Smith has been working while traveling around the U.S. and the world for the past two years with her husband, Erik, with only a carry-on suitcase and a laptop. They spend their time searching for delicious cheeses, a solid wifi connection and a decent cup of coffee.
